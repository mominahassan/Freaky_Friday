About

We are a small group of people who started the company Smooth Connection in 2019, and have come a long way since then. The purpose of our company has been
to showcase the work of Kriti Baniya. She is a unique artist with several achievements. We have therefore chosen to create a gallery dedicated to her,
where we will show all her work.

Artist

Kriti Baniya

Kriti Baniya founded the company "Smooth Connections" in the hope of sharing her work. Long has she been an artist who expresses her feelings
through her art. Kriti has achieved several things throughout her career. She has graduated from the Royal College of Art in the United Kingdom,
build an art school in Japan and the US, and at last, won the World Award of Arts in 2019. Her art is not just an object, but rather a
person with emotions, hopes and dreams. And with this, she wants people to feel connected and heard through her art.
